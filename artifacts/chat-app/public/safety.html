Your Safety Guide

Five simple rules to keep every conversation safe, respectful, and enjoyable on AnoChat.in.

1

Never Share Personal Information

Do not reveal your real name, phone number, home address, workplace, school, or any social media handles during a chat session. Anonymous connections are only anonymous if you keep them that way. Once shared, personal data cannot be taken back.

2

Report and Block Immediately

If another user sends you inappropriate content, makes you uncomfortable, or violates our community guidelines, use the Block button in the chat header immediately. You can also report a specific message using the flag icon. Our Grievance Officer reviews all reports within 36 hours (2 hours for urgent cases).

3

Treat Every Person With Respect

Harassment, hate speech, threats, or any form of abuse are strictly prohibited and will result in an immediate permanent ban. Your IP address is logged and serious violations may be reported to I4C or law enforcement authorities under the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS) 2023.

4

Never Share or Request Images

Do not share or request personal photographs, especially those that can be traced back to your real identity. The sharing of non-consensual intimate imagery (NCII) or AI-generated deepfakes is a criminal offence under IT Rules 2026 and will result in an immediate ban and law enforcement referral.

5

Be Aware of Scams and Manipulation

Be cautious of users who quickly build emotional rapport and then ask for money, gift cards, or personal favours. Do not click links sent by strangers. If a conversation feels engineered or too good to be true, trust your instincts, end the chat, and report the user.
