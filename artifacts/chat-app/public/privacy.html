Privacy Policy

We built AnoChat.in on a foundation of privacy. Here's exactly what we collect, why, and how we protect it.

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

AnoChat.in is designed to minimise data collection. We do not require email addresses, phone numbers, or social media logins. When you join a chat session, we collect:

  • Your chosen username (temporary, session-based)
  • Your IP address (for safety, moderation, and legal compliance)
  • Your gender, age, and country as declared by you
  • Message content (stored encrypted for moderation and legal compliance)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To connect you with other users in real time
  • To enforce our Community Guidelines and Safety Policy
  • To comply with Indian law, including IT Rules 2026 and BNS 2023
  • To respond to valid legal requests from law enforcement authorities

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third-party advertisers. AnoChat.in is ad-free.

3. Data Retention

Regular chat messages are stored for a limited session window. If a message is reported and removed under IT Rules 2026, the following data is retained for 180 days as required by law:

  • Message content
  • Sender IP address
  • Timestamp of the message

This data may be shared with I4C or law enforcement on receipt of a valid legal order.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser session storage (not persistent cookies) to maintain your active chat session. This data is cleared automatically when you close your browser tab. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

We may update this policy to reflect changes in law or our practices. Continued use of AnoChat.in after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. Significant changes will be highlighted on the homepage.
